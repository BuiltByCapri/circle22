Circle 22

What This Is

Circle 22 is a structured evening for single professionals who value thoughtful conversation and deliberate connection. It is not a dating app, a networking event, or an open mixer.

Each gathering is small, between 16 and 24 people, and designed around shared context: a theme, a question, or a format that rewards presence.

How It Works

You request an invitation through a brief form. We review each request individually and extend invitations based on alignment with the group.

Gatherings happen on a periodic basis. There are no tickets, no walk-ins, and no plus-ones.

Who This Is For

Circle 22 is designed for professionals in their 30s, 40s, and early 50s who are looking for something more considered than apps or large social events.

This is not for everyone. That’s intentional.
