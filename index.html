WHAT IS CIRCLE 22?

Circle 22 is a curated connection experience for Houston singles who are tired of apps and mixers.

We match people based on behavioral compatibility—not demographics, not photos, not where you went to school.

How you show up. Your energy and tempo. What draws you in. How you hold connection.

This is what happens when you stop optimizing for volume and start optimizing for compatibility.

THE COMPASS

The Compass is our behavioral matching system.

It measures your four core connection elements:

  • The Needle (How You Show Up)
  • The Rose (Energy & Tempo)
  • The Bearing (What Draws You In)
  • The Anchor (How You Hold Connection)

It takes 8-10 minutes. The results are yours to keep.

When applications for our first event open (Spring 2026), you'll take The Compass as part of the process.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Apply

We're not looking for a specific "type." We're looking for people who are intentional, open, and ready for something real.

2. Take The Compass

Our 22-question behavioral assessment reveals how you connect—not just who you are on paper.

3. Get matched

We curate the group based on compatibility. You'll meet people who match your energy, not just your demographics.

4. Show up

One evening. Pre-matched guests. Wine. Real conversation.

Chemistry's on you. Compatibility's on us.

WHO THIS IS FOR

Circle 22 is for Houston singles (32-52) who:

  • Value intention over volume
  • Are tired of apps and mixers
  • Believe chemistry requires more than a swipe
  • Are ready for something real

This is not for everyone. That's intentional.
