How It Works

Apply. If accepted, you'll complete Circle 22's Compass - our behavioral matching system built on 4 elements of human connection.

We use your responses to curate your evening. You'll meet six people who match your values, energy, and conversational style. Twenty seats total. Pre-matched. By invitation only.

Who This Is For

Professionals in their 30s, 40s, and early 50s who want something more deliberate than apps or mixers.

This is not for everyone. That's intentional.
